Here at Royse Lock & Hardware, we are happy to serve our customers with respect. Whether it's quickly opening a locked car door, restoring historic hardware, or rekeying a whole apartment building, our goal is to keep you safe, secure, and protected.


Based in Winchester, Clark County, KY, Royse Lock & Hardware is one of the only family owned locksmith businesses serving communities east of Lexington. We proudly cover Clark, Montgomery, Bourbon, Madison, Powell, and Estill counties — including Mt. Sterling, Paris, Richmond, and the surrounding region. We promise honest communication, fair pricing, and a commitment to the safety and security of every customer.
